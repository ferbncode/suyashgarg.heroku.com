What is Sentikart?

Sentikart is a hack that facilitates the world of online shopping. It is the outcome of our (Team Member: Utkarsh Raj) 7-day effort for the IndiaHacks-2016 Online Commerce Track. It won the 7th place in the nation and brought us good new Raspberry Pi to learn more things with. Sentikart scrapes the comments out of e-Commerce sites like FlipKart and runs a sentimental analysis algorithm on them to present the user a summary of the reviews. Not just a simple sentimental analysis. The algorithm manages to categorizes the comments into categories of the product and rates it in all those aspects. Other than that, it has a Q/A portal, a small service section. Here are a few screenshots from the webapp. Find the source code here.
